Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App using Flask & Machine Learning

In today's digital age, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more crucial than ever. With the growing need for remote healthcare solutions, we developed a symptom-based disease diagnosis web application. This project leverages Flask for the backend and a Decision Tree Classifier model to create a user-friendly platform that helps users identify potential illnesses based on their symptoms.

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and seek quick answers about their health concerns. However, differentiating between various diseases can be challenging, especially when symptoms overlap. Our goal was to build a convenient and accessible solution that allows users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses instantly.

The Solution

We designed a web app that enables users to enter symptoms and get a predicted disease using a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model. Hereâ€™s how the system works:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through an intuitive interface supporting a wide range of symptoms.
  2. Machine Learning Model: The Decision Tree Classifier, trained on a dataset of symptoms and diseases, predicts the most likely illness.
  3. Prediction: The app provides instant predictions based on input symptoms, helping users gain quick health insights.
  4. Additional Information: Users receive details about the predicted disease, including its description, precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: A clean, intuitive design ensures seamless user interaction.
  • Accurate Predictions: The ML model delivers reliable predictions based on symptom analysis.
  • Comprehensive Health Information: Users receive in-depth details on diseases, including treatment suggestions.
  • Educational Content: The platform features informative health-related articles to enhance awareness.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App harnesses the power of machine learning to provide users with valuable health insights. By making healthcare information more accessible and user-centric, we aim to enhance health awareness and empower individuals to take proactive steps toward their well-being.

In an era where remote healthcare is increasingly essential, this project represents a step forward in delivering quick, reliable, and AI-driven health diagnostics to the masses.
